# OF

## Aim:
The focus of this study is to observe & understand the differences, pros, & cons between various well-known optical flow methods.

## Introduction:
Optical Flow is the apparent motion of objects in an image between 2 consecutive frames caused by movement of an object or a camera in an environment. There are 2 types of optical flow estimation - *Sparse Estimation & Dense Estimation*.
